Click-Style

Click Style is a simple but good looking and helpful jQuery plugin. It styles the way a button looks when clicked automatically

TO USE: Put the jquery file and the css file(optional) into your website directory. In your jquery script add: $(button).clickStyle(); To change the parameters, do this: $(button).clickStyle({color: "white", background: "#29bf35",text: "Success!",rotate: "yes",transition: "1000",bordercolor: "none"}); Inside the quotation marks change it to your desires.
